How it works

From spat to plate, in four steps.

Every cage in the OYCI treasury moves through the same four stages. Each transition is signed by the farm operator and stamped on Injective.

Stage 01

Built

Marine-grade cage assembled onshore, numbered, minted on the OYCI contract.

Stage 02

Seeded

20,000 oyster spat introduced per cage. Genetic record committed on chain.

Stage 03

In the water

Submerged on a longline lease in the James River, Virginia for 18 months.

Stage 04

Harvested

Cage pulled, oysters graded, proceeds buy-back & burn $OYCI on the open market.
